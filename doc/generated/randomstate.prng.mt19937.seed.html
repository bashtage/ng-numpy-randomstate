randomstate.prng.mt19937.seed¶

randomstate.prng.mt19937.seed(seed=None)¶

Seed the generator.

This method is called when RandomState is initialized. It can be called again to re-seed the generator. For details, see RandomState.

Parameters
seed (int or array_like, optional) -- Seed for RandomState. Must be convertible to 32 bit unsigned integers.
